WebRTC samples Constraints & statistics

This demo shows ways to use constraints and statistics in WebRTC applications.

Click Capture to (re)open the camera with these constraints.

The lefthand video shows the output of getUserMedia(); on the right is the video after being passed through the local peer connection.

Below the videos is a running display of how many kb/s the video feed uses for transmission.
